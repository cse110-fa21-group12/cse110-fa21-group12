What is CREATE?

We’re all busy people. We know how hard it is to find the time to cook tasty homemade meals, let alone figure out what to cook, what ingredients to buy, and how much. With CREATE, we hope to ease some of this stress for you. By creating a free account, you can have a place to add your own recipes and discover new, awesome recipes from other users. It’s easy to search recipes to find the perfect meal that fits your mood and requirements. Also take advantage of our shopping list feature, which helps you keep track of what ingredients (and how much) you need to buy on your next grocery outing. Go ahead and sign up. You’ll thank us later.
